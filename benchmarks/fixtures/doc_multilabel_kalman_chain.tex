\section{Kalman derivative chain}
The filter recursions introduce several nearby quantities that should not be conflated when checking Hessian work.

\begin{equation}\label{eq:kalman-prediction-covariance}
P_{t|t-1} = T_t P_{t-1|t-1}T_t' + R_t Q_t R_t'.
\end{equation}

The innovation residual and covariance use the predicted moments.

\begin{equation}\label{eq:kalman-innovation-covariance}
S_t = Z_t P_{t|t-1}Z_t' + H_t.
\end{equation}

The score contribution depends on the innovation covariance, its derivative, and the innovation residual.

\begin{equation}\label{eq:kalman-score-contribution}
\partial_i \ell_t
= -\frac{1}{2}\operatorname{tr}(S_t^{-1}\partial_i S_t)
+ \frac{1}{2}v_t' S_t^{-1}(\partial_i S_t)S_t^{-1}v_t
- (\partial_i v_t)'S_t^{-1}v_t.
\end{equation}

A Hessian derivation differentiates this score contribution again. The surrounding covariance equations are dependencies, not proofs of the second derivative.
