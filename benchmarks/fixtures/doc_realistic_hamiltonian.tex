\section{Hamiltonian transport diagnostics}
The implementation should preserve the energy decomposition used by the sampler when reporting diagnostics.

\begin{proposition}[Hamiltonian energy decomposition]\label{prop:hamiltonian-energy}
The Hamiltonian is the sum of the potential energy and kinetic energy terms.
\end{proposition}

This diagnostic identity is used only for monitoring; it should not alter the transition kernel.
