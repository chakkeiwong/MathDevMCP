# Phase 03 Subplan: Executable Generic Obligation Engine

## Objective

Produce replayable deterministic checks for closed mathematical targets and
honest abstentions for ambiguous targets.

## Entry Conditions

Phase 02 graph and source anchors are available.

## Required Artifacts

Validator registry, trace records, bounded SymPy route, paired true/false
fixtures, and a Phase 03 result.

## Required Checks/Tests/Reviews

Test arithmetic, equality, level/log, explicit references, domains/tolerances,
malformed expressions, unsupported notation, and no arbitrary subprocesses.
Replay every finding from its trace and verify disposition conservation.

## Evidence Contract

A confirmed defect requires a closed target, assumptions/domain, deterministic
tool/version, and source anchors. Otherwise use `supported_tension` or
`not_checkable`.

## Forbidden Claims/Actions

No keyword hit or CAS simplification may be presented as an economic or causal
interpretation.

## Exact Handoff Conditions

The C.75/C.77/C.79-style relational fixtures generate findings or explicit
reasoned abstentions, with replayable traces.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if a validator cannot separate a closed target from a heuristic.
