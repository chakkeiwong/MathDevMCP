corpus-specific diagnostics, not population estimates.

answer-key exposure, permit source drift, rank by finding count, or mix agent
inference into tool-only scores.

## Exact Handoff Conditions

reason and next repair action.

## Stop Conditions

exposure is recorded as a blind-claim veto rather than an engineering stop.
