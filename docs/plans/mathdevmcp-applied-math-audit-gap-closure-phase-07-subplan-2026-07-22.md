# Phase 07 Subplan: Independent Review And Release Decision

## Objective

Red-team the implementation, evidence contract, documentation, and scientific
non-claims, then make a limited colleague-facing readiness decision.

## Entry Conditions

Phase 06 scorecard and all phase artifacts are complete.

## Required Artifacts

Review record, test summary, final remaining-gap report, master result, and
reset memo.

## Required Checks/Tests/Reviews

Run focused and relevant broad suites, MCP stdio smoke, diff/document review,
artifact hashes, and read-only independent review when available.

## Evidence Contract

Readiness is limited to demonstrated experimental scope; no scientific
correctness or complete recall is implied.

## Forbidden Claims/Actions

Do not call the system production-grade or mathematically complete. Do not
commit or push without an explicit separate request.

## Exact Handoff Conditions

The final report names closed gaps, unresolved gaps, evidence, and the next
repair direction.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if public-contract correctness or evidence integrity remains defective.
