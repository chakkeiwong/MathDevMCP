# Phase 03 Subplan: Source-First And Specialist Routing

## Objective

Use ResearchAssistant for PDF intake and mark specialist routes according to
actual source type, with DynareMCP optional rather than core.

## Entry Conditions

Phase 02 coverage ledger passes.

## Required Artifacts

Provider route records, optional specialist registry, Dynare `.mod` and
non-Dynare fixtures, and a phase result note.

## Required Checks

ResearchAssistant failure visibility, no-source fallback, `.mod` applicability,
non-Dynare `not_applicable`, and no specialist claim beyond its declared scope.

## Evidence Contract

Provider, source, agent, specialist, and runtime evidence remain separate.

## Forbidden Claims/Actions

Do not execute arbitrary commands, treat Dynare diagnostics as semantic proof,
or call missing specialists a scientific refutation.

## Handoff Conditions

Each route has a deterministic applicability decision, operation list, and
limitation/non-claim.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if provider errors are hidden or a specialist route lacks applicability
evidence.
