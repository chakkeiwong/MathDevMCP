# Phase 05 Subplan: Cross-Domain Regression And Boehl Re-Test

## Objective

Measure the orchestrator on small cross-domain fixtures and repeat the Boehl
PDF run without answer-key hints.

## Entry Conditions

Phase 04 public function and artifact resolution pass.

## Required Artifacts

Economics, finance, marketing/management, and PDF fixtures; run manifest;
benchmark coverage report; and a phase result note.

## Required Checks

Exact/partial/missed matching, false-positive review, abstention accounting,
source anchors, tool-only versus fresh-agent attribution, and source hash
stability.

## Evidence Contract

Metrics are descriptive for this corpus. A low score triggers repair and does
not invalidate a sound harness.

## Forbidden Claims/Actions

No answer-key access before blind artifact freeze; no tuning on the same blind
run; no general recall or superiority claim.

## Handoff Conditions

The benchmark reports what improved and which obligation families remain weak.

## Stop Conditions

Stop only the blind arm on leakage, source drift, or absent frozen output.
