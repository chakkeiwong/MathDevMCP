# Phase 00 Subplan: Baseline And Fixtures

Objective: freeze the first fresh result and all generic positive, negative,
ambiguous, renaming, distant-reference, adversarial-distractor, and cross-
domain fixtures for role, relation, sign/timing, ownership, coefficient, and
source-closure checks.

Entry: baseline artifact SHA-256 and post-hoc comparison are readable.

Artifacts: fixture records, source digests, baseline manifest, result note.

Checks/review: at least 12 cases (4 closed positive, 4 closed negative, 4
ambiguous), at least three domains and every relation family, no Boehl labels/
phrases, immutable answer record, focused baseline tests.

Evidence: fixtures validate engineering behavior, not scientific recall.

Forbidden: answer-key fixtures, score-driven rules, changed baseline files.

Handoff: every repair has a discriminating fixture and Phase 01 is reviewed.

Stop: baseline identity or fixture isolation fails.
