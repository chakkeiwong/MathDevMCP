# Phase 03 Subplan: Conservative Relations

Objective: require compatible roles, symbols, domains, and proximity for
candidate relations; preserve ambiguity.

Entry: Phase 02 typed candidates validate.

Artifacts: matcher, score explanation, negative/ambiguous tests, result note.

Checks/review: false-link confusion matrix, duplicate suppression, thresholds,
renaming/distant/adversarial/cross-domain cases, source-specific rule scan.

Evidence: inferred relations nominate checks; they do not establish them.

Forbidden: shared words or adjacency alone establishing dependency.

Handoff: false links are measured and ambiguous pairs abstain.

Stop: ambiguous relation promotes a defect.
