# Phase 08 Subplan: Required Red-Team And Closeout

## Objective

Independently review code, tests, evidence, replay protocol, and non-claims;
state residual scientific and engineering gaps.

## Entry Conditions

Phase 07 replay artifacts and post-hoc comparison are frozen.

## Required Artifacts

Independent review record, relevant-test summary, final result, reset memo,
artifact hash inventory, and a next-program recommendation if gaps remain.

## Required Checks/Tests/Reviews

Run focused and relevant broad tests, stable/all MCP stdio smokes, compile and
diff checks, artifact hash verification, source-specific-rule scan, and one
independent read-only reviewer. The reviewer must not be the implementation or
replay agent and must inspect the final code/evidence rather than merely the
plan.

## Evidence Contract

Fixture results are engineering regressions, not sealed holdout evidence. The
same-paper replay is tuned and descriptive. Protocol non-access is self-
reported rather than OS-enforced. Readiness is limited to demonstrated
experimental scope.

## Forbidden Claims/Actions

Do not claim scientific correctness, general recall, product generalization,
production readiness, enforced replay isolation, or an unbiased holdout.

## Exact Handoff Conditions

The final result names closed, narrowed, unchanged, and newly exposed gaps;
lists all failed or unrun checks; separates engineering, mathematical, source,
and scientific ledgers; and provides the next smallest justified program.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if the public contract, artifact identity, replay attribution, or evidence
integrity remains defective.
