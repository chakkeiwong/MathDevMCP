## Mission

Make MathDevMCP exploratory in mathematical search and rigorous at the claim
boundary on a real, versioned academic document. Every relevant public
workflow must bind the same exact source object, preserve its relation and
source-evidenced routing role, generate locally relevant obligations, execute
supported external-tool checks, retain replayable evidence, and abstain
precisely when mathematical or empirical evidence is unavailable.

This program repairs the system. It does not edit or validate the economic
content of the v8 source document.

## Corrected Baseline

- All 65 registered MCP tools are applicability-accounted: 56 invoked and 9
  inapplicable without inventing code, literature, or temporal inputs.
- Exact source binding already works in proof/negative packets, audit/fix, and
  document-tree entry points, but proof substatus and downstream consumers can
  contradict that binding.
- Equality-style targets work for seven representative labels. Conditional
  expectation and conditional independence are currently quarantined.
- The seeded benchmark comparator passes but does not exercise these real-
  document defects and is explanatory only.
- The previous clean full-suite comparator was 1593 passed and 4 skipped. The
  current worktree contains the uncommitted v8 audit artifacts and they must be
  preserved.
- Publication is disabled and remains outside this program's authority.

## Execution Protocol

1. Audit the active subplan against its predecessor result and current diff.
2. State or confirm its phase evidence contract before nontrivial commands.
3. Implement the smallest shared-contract change that closes the phase vetoes.
4. Run focused tests, then adjacent surface/order tests.
5. Inspect the diff and write the phase result with evidence, residual risks,
   decision table, and non-claims.
6. Refresh and audit the next subplan from actual state; launch automatically
   when entry conditions pass.
7. Repair ordinary failures locally. Pause only for a human/scientific,
   permission, cost, destructive, publication, release, or project-direction
   boundary.

## Global Forbidden Actions And Claims

- Do not edit v8, enable publication, install packages, push, or authorize a
  release.
- Do not infer mathematical truth from a source routing role.
- Do not treat a definition, identity, retrieval hit, route plan, skeleton,
  CAS simplification, or proof-state trace as proof beyond its exact scope.
- Do not claim randomization occurred, LATE is identified, a terminal value is
  economically valid, or a Bellman policy is optimal without the required
  evidence.
- Do not introduce in-house proof/search algorithms without an external-tool
  gap justification.
- Do not erase or rewrite unrelated worktree changes or predecessor evidence.

## Program Stop Conditions

- The frozen source digest changes.
- A phase cannot preserve exact source/relation/role identity.
- A deterministic backend result cannot be distinguished from scientific or
  certifying evidence.
- Evidence replay accepts stale source, altered assumptions, or cross-label
  substitution.
- Required changes cross source-edit, publication, release, dependency-install,
  external disclosure, or destructive boundaries.
- A material scientific interpretation remains unresolved after safe local
  investigation.
