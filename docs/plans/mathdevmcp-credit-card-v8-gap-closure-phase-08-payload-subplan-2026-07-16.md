# Phase 08 Subplan: Agent Payload And Resolver Repair

## Phase Objective

Make decisive outputs practically consumable without deleting or weakening
complete evidence.

## Entry Conditions Inherited From Previous Phase

- Detailed evidence is current and replayable.
- Existing raw packet/report sizes reproduce the predecessor audit.

## Required Artifacts

- Compact proof and negative packets no larger than 30,720 bytes.
- Compact tree below 25,600 bytes and bounded rigor/tree Markdown summaries.
- Digest-bound resolver for exact detailed packets and per-label sections.
- CLI/facade/FastMCP byte and semantic parity tests.
- `mathdevmcp-credit-card-v8-gap-closure-phase-08-result-2026-07-16.md`.

## Required Checks

- Resolver round trips reproduce exact detailed bytes.
- Compact and detailed views share status, source, relation, role, assumptions,
  vetoes, backend evidence, selected action, promotion boundary, and non-claims.
- Payload budgets hold on all nine v8 labels and public envelopes.

## Evidence Contract

- Pass: transport views meet budgets while exact evidence remains resolvable.
- Veto: compaction changes a status, drops a veto/non-claim, or fabricates
  completeness.
- Non-claim: smaller output does not improve mathematical validity.

## Forbidden Claims And Actions

- Do not truncate canonical targets or source identity to meet a byte budget.
- Do not discard detailed artifacts after compaction.

## Exact Next-Phase Handoff Conditions

Phase 09 may start when all decisive compact views pass semantic and resolver
parity.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if the budget can be met only by dropping claim-boundary evidence; record
the smallest honest exceeded-boundary result instead.
