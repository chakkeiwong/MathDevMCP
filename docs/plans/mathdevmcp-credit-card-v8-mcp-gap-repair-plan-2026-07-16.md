## Objective

Repair the source-selection, relation-extraction, role, assumption, backend,
evidence-binding, and agent-payload failures exposed by the v8 audit. The goal
is not to force more `verified` statuses. The goal is to make every public
workflow consume the same exact mathematical object, produce role-appropriate
obligations, execute supported specialist routes, and abstain precisely where
the evidence remains insufficient.

## Skeptical Plan Audit

### Wrong order risk

Adding probability, causal, or Bellman formalizers before unifying source
selection and relation identity would encode inconsistent or truncated
targets. Phases 01-03 therefore repair the shared contract before any new
mathematical adapter.

### Status inflation risk

Replacing `source_label_missing` with `unverified` is not sufficient by itself.
The repair must prove that exact source identity is accepted while separately
recording parser/backend readiness. No status change is a promotion criterion.

### Role overreach risk

Source prose can evidence that an equation is intended as an identity,
definition, estimand, or assumption. It cannot by itself prove that the
identity is exhaustive, the definition is economically valid, the estimand is
identified, or the assumption holds. Role authority controls routing only.

### Formalization scope risk

Conditional expectation, LATE, randomization, and Bellman targets require
different mathematics. A single generic CAS adapter would be misleading.
Adapters must be target-family-specific and retain non-claims.

### Benchmark proxy risk

Existing 70/70 seeded benchmarks did not detect the v8 failures. New regression
fixtures must use a versioned duplicate-label corpus and the exact relation
shapes. Passing seeded tests still will not establish broad scientific validity.

### Audit decision

The program is feasible if implemented in contract-first order with v8
negative controls, exact source digests, and veto-first promotion. No package
installation, source edit, publication, or external model service is required.

## Phase 01: Exact Source Selection Everywhere

### Objective

Add exact `file` and `source_digest` selectors to assumption and derivation
report workflows and make ambiguity/stale-source outcomes explicit.

### Required changes

- propagate selectors through library, CLI, facade, server, and schemas;
- use the validated label-scoped obligation rather than
  `index["labels"].get(label)`;
- distinguish `ambiguous_label`, `source_digest_mismatch`, and `label_absent`;
- preserve unique-label backward compatibility.

### Tests

- v8 duplicate-corpus assumption and derivation reports inspect all requested
  targets with exact selectors;
- unqualified duplicates fail visibly;
- stale digest fails closed;
- exact target digests match proof audit and audit/fix.

### Handoff

Phase 02 begins only when no exact selected v8 label is reported absent or
silently taken from a sibling file.

## Phase 02: Parser Status And Provenance Separation

### Objective

Stop directory-wide parser-policy ambiguity from overwriting an already
validated exact-source extraction.

### Required changes

- pass exact file/digest and expected selected labels into parser policy;
- separate `source_binding_status` from `specialist_parser_readiness`;
- replace `inconclusive:source_label_missing` with a truthful scoped substatus
  when source identity is accepted but certification is not ready;
- keep duplicate-corpus diagnostics in a separate corpus ledger.

### Tests

- all nine exact v8 packets record accepted source identity;
- true missing labels retain `source_label_missing`;
- exact extraction plus context-only parser remains non-certifying without
  claiming the source label is missing;
- audit/fix and negative packets inherit the corrected status.

### Handoff

Phase 03 begins only when status text, provenance, and next action agree.

## Phase 03: Canonical Relation And Role Contract

### Objective

Extend the canonical obligation beyond equality to common mathematical
relations and carry a closed source-evidenced role through every consumer.

### Required relation forms

- equality/definition/identity;
- scalar expression object without equality;
- conditional independence with conditioning set;
- inequality/feasibility constraint;
- optimization/argmax or max recursion;
- estimand ratio with denominator obligation.

### Required role forms

- definition;
- accounting identity;
- policy/value recursion;
- causal estimand/object;
- identification assumption;
- statistical estimator;
- theorem/proposition;
- diagnostic expression.

Only exact source context may grant routing authority. Caller assertions remain
diagnostic.

### Tests

- `eq:causal-cashflow-object` and `eq:randomization-assumption` extract with
  exact spans and typed relation ASTs;
- all nine labels have source-supported roles;
- a generic unsupported expression retains theorem/default behavior;
- role conflict or ambiguous prose fails closed.

### Handoff

Phase 04 begins only when the nine labels share one source/relation/role
identity across proof audit, rigor, tree, packets, and report APIs.

## Phase 04: Rigor And Fix Consumer Unification

### Objective

Remove the lightweight locator and substring role classifier from decisive
rigor/fix paths.

### Required changes

- use complete canonical targets for multiline `align` rows;
- classify roles from the Phase 03 contract, not keywords such as `\delta`;
- ensure gap location and proof target refer to the same canonical obligation;
- reject proof targets like `\begin{equation}`;
- report coverage for every requested label, including safe abstentions.

### Tests

- cash-flow, stock-flow, and Bellman targets are complete and identical across
  all reports;
- randomization is an identification assumption, not valuation;
- causal object and stock-flow identity are classified correctly;
- audit/fix returns nine evidence ledger rows or explicit per-label abstentions.

### Handoff

Phase 05 begins only after cross-surface parity holds for target and role.

## Phase 05: Role-Specific Obligation Builders

### Objective

Generate local obligations appropriate to each mathematical role instead of
attaching generic NPV requirements to unrelated identities.

### Required builders

- accounting identity: component definitions, signs, units, timing, and
  exhaustiveness local to the identity;
- terminal definition: exact denominator, scalar roles, sensitivity, and
  definition/economic boundary;
- conditional expectation: probability space/kernel, conditioning object,
  measurability, and integrability;
- LATE: nonzero first stage, instrument assignment, exclusion, monotonicity,
  SUTVA/no interference as scoped, and local complier interpretation;
- randomization assumption: assignment mechanism, eligible population,
  randomization unit, interference/override diagnostics, and lineage;
- Bellman: state/action domain, feasible-set nonemptiness, transition kernel,
  reward/value finiteness, horizon/boundary, and policy measurability.

### Negative controls

- no baseline/terminal-value obligation on the local PD/LGD/EAD identity;
- no matrix-conformability obligation from `\star` notation;
- no causal-identification conclusion from a predictive equation.

### Handoff

Phase 06 begins only when obligation relevance tests pass before backend work.

## Phase 06: Specialist External-Tool Execution

### Objective

Execute the smallest supported deterministic route and feed the result back
into the branch state without overclaiming.

### Routes

- SymPy: terminal-value denominator/cross-multiplication and supported scalar
  accounting normalization;
- Sage/SymPy: optional exact algebra checks where the typed relation is scalar;
- Lean: certification only for explicit placeholder-free formal statements;
- LeanSearch/LeanExplore: premise retrieval only after formalization;
- jixia: Lean static extraction, not certification;
- Pantograph/LeanDojo: proof-state/search interaction only when installed and
  toolchain-compatible.

### Required integration

- template auto-selection must match exact decorated terminal-value LaTeX;
- valuation validation must attach to proof/tree/fix packets;
- backend results must update branch evidence, not remain sidecar files;
- unavailable tools remain engineering diagnostics.

### Handoff

Phase 07 begins when at least the terminal-value slice and one accounting
identity execute end to end, while unsupported causal/policy targets abstain
with exact next evidence.

## Phase 08: Agent Payload And Resolver Repair

### Objective

Make all decisive outputs consumable without deleting evidence.

- digest-bound resolver for complete packets and per-label report sections;
- preserve source, role, status, assumptions, vetoes, selected action, backend

- lower compact tree output below both canonical and transport targets.

### Tests

- byte budgets on CLI, facade, and FastMCP envelopes;
- resolver round trips reproduce exact detailed bytes;
- compact/detailed views have identical status and promotion boundaries.

### Handoff

Phase 09 begins only when all nine-label summaries are practically consumable.

## Forbidden Claims And Actions

- Do not infer role from equality syntax or keywords alone.
- Do not call an exact accepted source label missing because sibling files
  duplicate it.
- Do not treat a definition or identity as a theorem without an explicit
  source-supported claim boundary.
- Do not treat a LATE formula as identified from algebra alone.
- Do not treat a randomization assumption as established by being stated.
- Do not certify Bellman or causal semantics with CAS simplification.
- Do not add in-house proof search without an external-tool gap justification.
- Do not edit v8, enable publication, install packages, or authorize release.

## Stop Conditions

- Stop if source identity changes.
- Stop a formalizer if its exact relation, role, types, or assumptions are not
  bound.
- Stop promotion on any parity, replay, source, role, or backend-evidence veto.
- Record unavailable external tools without treating absence as refutation.
- If a phase exposes a broader architecture issue, preserve a minimal v8
  regression and revise this plan before expanding scope.

## Completion Conditions

The program is complete when all nine phases have recorded outcomes, exact v8
source/relation/role identity is shared across public workflows, supported
external routes feed evidence back into the main loop, outputs are bounded and
resolvable, the full suite and repeated v8 audit have run, and the result states
all remaining gaps without crossing scientific or publication boundaries.
