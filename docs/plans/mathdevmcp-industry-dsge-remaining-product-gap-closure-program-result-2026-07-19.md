original 106-page dossier.

## Remaining Gaps

1. Role and exposition cue accuracy is not measured on an independently labelled
   scholarly corpus.
2. Candidate patch precision and usefulness are not measured by blinded human
   editorial review.
3. The original large dossier was not rerun as an independent generalization
   test in this program.
4. Motivation, narrative arc, cognitive load, typography, rendered-PDF quality,
   and reader comprehension remain outside MathDevMCP's certifying scope.
5. Mathematical and source-specific correctness still require derivation,
   citation inspection, or certifying backend evidence for the exact claim.

## Post-Run Red Team

The strongest alternative explanation is that the deterministic fixture cues
fit the reviewed examples but do not generalize. The result would be overturned
for a product-default claim by a provenance-clean corpus showing unacceptable
false closure, false role assignment, or unsafe candidate wording. The weakest
evidence is independent semantic accuracy; the strongest evidence is the
source-bound transport, lifecycle, metadata, and negative-control behavior.
