## Executive Decision

`BOUNDED_V8_LANE_ACCOMPLISHED`

The nine-label v8 lane is now coherent as an exploratory, agent-facing
mathematical-development workflow with a rigorous claim boundary. Its public
consumers select the same exact source bytes, preserve the same mathematical
relation and source-evidenced routing role, generate role-local obligations,
run the supported specialist checks, retain replayable evidence, and abstain
when the source and available tools do not establish a result.

This is an engineering and evidence-orchestration result. It is not a verdict
that the document, the nine equations, or their economic and causal claims are
correct. All proof and negative-evidence packets remain `unverified`, seven of
nine specialist routes abstain, publication remains disabled, and the audit
covers nine selected labels rather than the entire corpus.

All 65 registered MCP tools are applicability-accounted. Fifty-six were
invoked and nine were inapplicable because the source does not provide the
required code, literature mapping, or temporal inputs. Invocation accounting
is operational evidence only; it is not a mathematical success rate.

## Remaining Gaps

### Scientific and mathematical gaps

1. No source-faithful proof establishes the panel NPV functional or its
   conditional expectation as well defined under verified assumptions.
2. The source does not establish that the randomization mechanism actually
   generated the claimed conditional independence.
3. The LATE expression is not identified until the first-stage, independence,
   exclusion, monotonicity, SUTVA, and complier-scope conditions are sourced or
   otherwise verified.
4. The Bellman display has not been formalized or proved as an optimality
   equation for the stated state, action, transition, reward, and boundary
   objects.
5. Terminal-value algebra is consistent under its scoped definition, but the
   denominator domain, units, sensitivity policy, and economic extrapolation
   are not established.
6. The local accounting identities still require source definitions and
   sign/unit/timing reconciliation. A syntactic or structural identity is not
   evidence that the components are correctly measured.
7. All nine proof and negative-evidence packets remain `unverified`; no
   certifying backend result supports a stronger claim.

These are honest residuals, not engineering failures. Closing them requires
new source evidence, formalization, data/design records, citations, or human
scientific judgment rather than another parser or report-format patch.

### Coverage and usability limits

- Nine labels do not establish behavior across the other labeled equations or
  whole-document prose.
- The detailed assumption and derivation Markdown reports remain verbose
  (about 31 KB and 59 KB). Decisive packet/tree/rigor surfaces are compact and
  detailed records are resolvable, but further offline report deduplication
  would improve scanability.
- Audit/fix now audits all nine exact labels without malformed targets and
  emits no unsafe edit, but its Markdown synthesizes one generic formalization
  gap. The role-specific assumption and tree reports remain the more complete
  per-label handoff.
- Broad-corpus generalization, release readiness, and publication readiness
  were not evaluated by this selected-label program.

## Post-Run Red Team

The strongest alternative explanation is that the apparent improvement is
overfit to nine registered labels and source prose that happens to make their
roles easy to classify. That is a real coverage limitation and prevents a
broad-corpus claim. It does not explain away the exact comparator evidence for
these nine labels.

The result would be overturned if replay failed after source or assumption
mutation, a public consumer selected a different target/relation/role, a
specialist result escaped its diagnostic boundary, or publication became
enabled. Those checks pass in the final run. The weakest evidence remains
scientific rather than engineering: seven specialist abstentions and no
certifying proof for any selected document claim.

## Non-Claims

- Tool invocation and passing tests are not mathematical evidence.
- Typed abstention is not refutation.
- A source-evidenced role is not assumption truth.
- A CAS diagnostic is not an economic, causal, or whole-document proof.
- No edit to v8 is proposed or authorized by this result.
