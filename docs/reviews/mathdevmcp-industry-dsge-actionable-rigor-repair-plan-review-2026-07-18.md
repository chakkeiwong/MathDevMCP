Verdict: `PASS_AFTER_REVISION`

## Findings Resolved Before Execution

1. The first draft treated compact reporting as an early deliverable. That could
   make wrong findings merely shorter. Structural localization, role routing,
   and context closure now precede statuses and reporting.
2. A positive repaired fixture alone could be satisfied by suppressing every
   inverse finding. The missing-condition fixture is now a hard false-closure
   veto.
3. Nearby prose cannot certify the matrix theorem. The plan now distinguishes
   “the document states a sufficient condition” from backend-certified truth.
4. A blanket removal of numeric diagnostics would regress executable audit use.
   Suppression is limited to symbolic exposition without numeric artifacts or an
   implementation target.
5. A single-role registry cannot describe a definition plus conditional
   identity. The plan requires a multi-role exposition projection while
   preserving the existing source-routing authority record.
6. Raw route records remain useful forensic evidence. The plan aggregates them
   for the human issue ledger but does not delete them from detailed evidence.
7. Existing production files contain uncommitted resumability work. The plan now
   requires working with those changes and validating the combined surface,
   rather than overwriting or reverting it.
8. The final commit request could accidentally stage another agent's `skills/`
   work or feedback memo. Those paths are explicitly excluded unless ownership
   changes before commit.

## Feasibility

The repository already has:

- exact label and byte-span extraction;
- paragraph-context extraction;
- source-bound role cue infrastructure;
- high-level audit/fix and rigor result contracts;
- compact artifact persistence;
- positive and negative regression fixtures.

The repair can therefore be implemented as a deterministic exposition-semantic
projection over existing evidence. No new theorem prover or model dependency is
needed.

## Boundary Review

- Mathematical proof and source truth remain outside context-closure authority.
- Candidate wording remains human-reviewed and non-certifying.
- General readability, motivation, pedagogy, PDF rendering, and publication are
  not claimed.
- The plan changes a product-facing workflow but not a release/default policy;
  the substantive post-execution review and full suite are sufficient before the
  requested commit and push.

## Approval

The plan is internally consistent, feasible, artifact-complete, and ready for
execution. Any false closure of the negative fixture, regression in explicit
numeric diagnostics, or loss of detailed evidence invalidates the close.
